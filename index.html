About

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate with the Tsinghua University SIGS, advised by Prof. Xinlei Chen, I'm also a jointly cultivated PhD student at the UC Berkeley. Prior to that, I received my B.Eng. degree with highest honor in Computer Science and Engineering from Central South University in 2022, advised by Prof. Ju Ren.
